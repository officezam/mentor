What to expect after you join

We want to ensure that your mentorship experience is a successful one. Before you sign up, here are a few things you can expect.

1

16 Week Commitment

Joining the CommunAbility e-Mentorship Program requires a 16 week commitment. Mentor-mentee pairs meet online through a live chat application every other week for a minimum of 20-30 minutes. Watch this quick video to find out more about the program cycle.

2

Eligibility Criteria

Our e-mentorship program is for and about people with disabilities. To participate, a mentee must be 18 years or older and attending a recognized college or university program in Ontario.

3

Screening and Training

As part of the application process, we carefully screen potential mentees to ensure they are the right fit for our e-mentorship program. Mentees must complete online training tailored to their role.

4

Evaluation Research

As a mentee, you will be invited to participate in research about your mentorship experience in the program; you may choose to not participate in the study.
