LEARNING MODULES

Our series of e-learning modules offer a convenient way to learn about topics related to disability and post-secondary education.

Each module is adaptable and designed for ease of use. Our modules are screen-reader friendly, and learners can increase or decrease text size and change colours to suit their needs. Learners with a CommunAbility account can pause any module and return to complete the module at their convenience.

The list of topics covered is growing, so check back often!

MENTORSHIP MODULE

This module focuses on mentorship and mentoring relationships. By following the story of Priya, a student, and Jennifer, a business professional, users will learn about how to successfully engage in a mentoring relationship from the perspectives of both a mentee and mentor. Once completed, learners will receive a Certificate of Completion.
