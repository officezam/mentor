WEBINARS

CommunAbility offers a series of webinars hosted through YouTube Live Streaming to help support students with disabilities navigate the post-secondary education environment. Featuring influential and interesting people, a variety of topics are discussed to provide valuable information to students, educators, professionals, and others. Viewers can participate in live webinars and submit comments and questions that they would like discussed. All past webinars also are archived in our library for viewing anytime.

LIVE WEBINARS

To attend a live webinar simply visit our webinar page on the date and time of the webinar and watch the live stream. Want to interact with presenters and other attendees? Follow the link below to our YouTube channel to watch, text and audio/video chat.

ON DEMAND WEBINARS

To access one of the on-demand webinars below, click on the title of the webinar to play the recording. Presentation materials used during a webinar also are available for you to download and use. To download the presentation pdf file, click on the link below each webinar.
